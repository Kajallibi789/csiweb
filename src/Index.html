Computer Society of India

Our society is dedicated to promoting the development and growth of coding skills among students. Our goal is to create a community of coders who can share their knowledge, collaborate on projects, and participate in coding competitions. Our society is open to all students who have an interest in coding, regardless of their academic background or level of experience.

Workshops

We organize workshops on various coding topics, including programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, and web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Hackathons

We organize hackathons, where participants work on real-world coding projects over a period of 24-48 hours. These events provide a great opportunity for participants to apply their coding skills to real-world problems, collaborate with other coders, and win prizes.

Coding Competitions

We participate in coding competitions organized by other institutions and also organize our own coding competitions. These competitions are a great way for participants to test their coding skills and win prizes.
